# Login Method

We want to make it easy for you to create an account and start selling. Our setup process is extremely simple. 

Log on using your Gmail SSO account without having to create a seperate account from your main e-mail. Use your e-mail to register to Source and gain direct access to our dashboard.

![Source Login](/login.png)

If you do not have a Google address you can always log in using any other e-mail & completing the basic signing on process - e-mail -password -name.
